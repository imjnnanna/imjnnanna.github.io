Music

Viola

I am currently a violist in the Yale Symphony Orchestra.

I've been playing viola since I was in seventh grade. In 2023, I performed the Walton Viola Concerto with the Wallingford Symphony Orchestra as the winner of the 2023 Mitchell Family Fund String Concerto Competition.

Other prizes I've won include first place at the US International Open Music Competition and the KAMSA Young Artist Competition, and second place at the American Protégé International Piano & String Competition.

As an ensemble player, I served as the principal violist of the Choate Symphony Orchestra all four years, and was the co-principal violist at the Connecticut Southern Regions Festival.


Carillon

Catch me playing carillon on the Harkness Towers! I'm part of the Yale University Guild of Carilloneurs.


Composition

I composed classical music since I was 11 years old, and pretty much stopped when I was 15, only intermittently writing pieces (& unrecorded) since then. Find past recordings here.

Unfortunately unrecorded, I premiered my full orchestra piece "Persephone" in 2019 with the California Philharmonic Youth Orchestra. I attended the Tanglewood Institute in Summer 2021 and the Curtis Institute Young Artist Summer Program in 2022 for composition studies.
